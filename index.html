title: My goal
date: 2021-06-20 18:04:23
tags: My goal
The goal I set myself was mainly about the score that I wanted to achieve for my IELTS reading. This was because at the beginning of my IELTS courses last summer, I not only could not understand the meaning of articles, but also always run out of time since I only could finish two reading tasks instead of three till the end of the exam, which led to low scores. So, at that time, I set a goal for myself, which was to strive to complete the task within the specified time and get a score of 7 points.
In order to achieve this goal, my first task was to memorize words to improve my vocabulary. Everyday I recited 50 new words and wrote them for memory. In addition, I also insisted on doing 3 to 4 practice questions every week to improve my skills and speed. This series of training has lasted for nearly half a year until now, I believe that I will persist until the examination room.
Many people have helped me during this time, but the most important one was my reading teacher who is very experienced and rigorous. Every time we finish a test in class, he will highlight everyone’s mistakes and let us translate the article ourselves, so that we can learn from our mistakes and broaden our knowledge. He also offered techniques and strategies on how to complete each task successfully. Without his detailed explanations, I could not master the method of answering reading questions. Another person I asked for help was my best friend Carol, who has helped me recently finished her IELTS test and got a good score of 9. So when I do the topics now, as soon as I have any problems I will turn to her for help immediately, and she is always willing to help me.
This taught me that most success comes from hard work, and only long term focus and hard work can lead to success.

I am Chen Hanqing from Nanjing, China, and I graduated from Nanjing Jinling High School A-level Centre.
When other people talk about me, the first word they may mention is kind since I’m always willing to help others when they are in need. My family members say that I’m a little careless in daily life but in some important events like final exams, I always show a sense of careful and reliable. In addition, I think I am a girl who is responsible because I often do what others ask me to do to the best.
The thing that impressed me most is that I once chose to be a volunteer in our school sports meeting . My work included taking photos of the athletes in our class, picking up rubbish in the playground, and preventing non athletes from entering the field. On the day of the sports meeting, I wore the little red cap of volunteers and held a camera,in order to capture the athletes’ wonderful moments, I ran back and forth in the playground. Although it was tiring, I didn’t complain about that as that was a chance for me to rise a challenge.

SMC (Senior Mathematical challenge) is a British mathematics competition held by ukmt (the UK mathematics trust), in which the Cambridge class takes part in the advanced category. The examination time is 90 minutes, and all the questions are multiple-choice questions. In 2020, due to the impact of the epidemic, competitions will be conducted online in all regions except the UK.

Ukmt SMC is an offline paper exam in the past years, which has been carried out in Cambridge class of international department for many years.

This test was very difficult, although I only got the silver medal, but I think I still got a good result.

At Christmas, I participated in a market activity called COLOR SPACE, and served as a day’s stall owner. COLOR SPACE is a creative market that combines new retail elements. The design of the market, the selection of brands and the whole picture are all derived from the results of the collision of ideas between the EV team and the students. It brings consumers a different level of experience.

This is a picture of me when I was sorting out the products.

[](

[https://imgchr.com/i/rzuF8s)

These are the goods I sold.

Through this market activity, I feel that it is not easy to make money. When selling, the communication with people and the price of goods can affect the quantity of sales. Through one day’s investigation and research, I found that people passing through the stalls are usually attracted by items with more bright colors. In addition, the same item sold more at the price of 29 yuan than at the price of 31 yuan. This may be an illusion of the price.

All in all, I think this activity is very meaningful and impressive.
