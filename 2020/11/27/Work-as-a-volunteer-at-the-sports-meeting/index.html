Work as a volunteer at the sports meeting

In this year’s sports meeting, I am honored to serve as the only volunteer in our class. My work includes taking pictures of the athletes in our class, picking up rubbish on the playground, and preventing non athletes from entering the field. On the day of the sports meeting, I wore the little red cap of volunteers and held a camera， in order to capture the wonderful moments of athletes, I ran back and forth in the playground.
